<p>
  Here are the instructions to use this app. After enetring thre app, you can move around the veiwport, by dragging ont he screen. You may then click on the red colored icon, and this will move you to that location just like a fist person point of view. After which you may click the back button to come back to the orbital controls. Now at top right you will find the search bar, where you can search for the book that you wish to search in PK Kelkar library. After which you will see the location of shelf in 3D along with the route to that shelf from different startingpoint. You may click on it and the red icon will guide you to the appropriate shelf. You will also find the option to find other facilities in librayr along the way, or you can just click on the yellow colored option at the bottom right to get to some other option like how to use the app and things like
